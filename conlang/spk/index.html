Xvilkom aParoli Kiusakenam

Welcome to Paroli Kiusakenam, or the Meerhagen Speak! This is one of the 2 languages that exists on Insular Sekronia in r/MapOfToulmond, and is different from Se Kijusakjenxe, which is phonologically and morphologically much more similar to the sinitic languages than Paroli Kiusakenam. That said, PK can still technically be classed as a descendant of SKSX, since chronologically PK comes after SKSX.
