Velkom ti Ikibimi

The Ikibimi script is relatively simple, since the unrealised language of Ikibimi has a relatively narrow phonological inventory. That said, it is also very restrictive. For example, the name Aster cannot be written in Ikibimi - it is a non-rhotic language, that is to say it doesn't contain r-sounding sounds. Don't ask me what it means, even professional linguists have no idea. Ikibimi is also a very angular script. At least, that's how I see it.
The stuff that you see above the words "AsterLP" on the left? Yeah, that spells "AstyLP". Sorry.
